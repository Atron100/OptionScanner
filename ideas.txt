Since the goal is to build a professional-grade options platform, I'd expand the specification with:

- Wheel Strategy (CSP → CC automation)
- Portfolio Margin & Reg-T calculations
- Early assignment risk estimation
- Ex-dividend detection (important for Covered Calls)
- Earnings calendar integration
- Volatility regime detection (High IV / Low IV)
- Automatic strategy selection based on market conditions
- Position adjustment engine (rolls, defensive adjustments)
- Historical performance database
- Strategy backtesting framework
- Monte Carlo simulations
- Portfolio optimization
- IBKR order management (preview first, execution later)
- AI-based trade ranking and explanations

One additional idea that I think would make this project stand out is to build it as an 
Options Research Platform, not just a scanner. That means every strategy 
(Iron Condor, CSP, CC, PMCC, Double Diagonal, etc.) would share the same analytics engine, 
optimizer, and reporting system. New strategies would then be simple plugins rather than 
requiring changes throughout the application.

That architecture will scale much better as the project grows.
